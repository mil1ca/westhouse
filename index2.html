About us

West House Distillery although young, is a company that creates high-quality, hand-crafted spirits in small batches.

Specializing in the production of outstanding gins, we are dedicated to detail in every step of production, using only the finest ingredients from different regions of Croatia.

Our mission transcends the production of beverages; we strive to create unforgettable experiences for every gin lover who chooses West House Distillery.

We proudly promote the "Made in Slavonia" label, highlighting the richness and authenticity of our local climate. Every bottle of our gin bears the seal of pride of Slavonia, and every sip reflects our passion and commitment to top quality.

We invite you to explore our refined collection of gins and let West House Distillery draw you into a world of exquisite flavors and aromatic experiences, created with love and care.

Awards

In accordance with our commitment to quality and details, we are pleased to inform our valued customers and partners that we were awarded at the prestigious ZGrappa event - the international brandy, liqueur and gin festival, which took place on October 13, 2023, at Boćarski dom in Zagreb.

At the 5th CROSPIRIT - international evaluation in a competition of 237 samples from the entire region, our gin stood out, winning the prestigious award for the best design and the silver award for quality.

This recognition is a reflection of our constant creativity and striving to make every aspect of our product second to none - we believe that product quality and design appeal go hand in hand in creating an unforgettable experience for our customers.

Products

Buclia Gin

Buklija is not only a bottle of drink, but also a symbol of Slavonic tradition that the groom uses as an invitation for his closest relatives to the wedding party. Inspired by this intangible cultural heritage of ours, the idea to call our gin Buclia was born.

Made from premium grain distillate, combined with carefully selected herbal ingredients, it creates a unique blend of flavors. Each bottle of Buclia gin contains six key botanicals, including essential pine, coriander, angelica, sage, Vis lemon and delicate rose. Late additions of fresh lemon peel and rose give it an aromatic and refreshing finish, perfect for any occasion.

Buclia gin is not just a drink, but an experience that conveys the richness of our culture and tradition.
